I am a Digital Marketing Specialist currently living in Seattle, WA. My specialties include photography, videography, copywriting/editing, and coding. I am currently the Communications Coordinator at Bellevue Arts Museum in beautiful downtown Bellevue, WA.

Photography

I am a fan of both digital and film photography, but I work primarily with a digital Nikon D5300 with a 50mm prime lens. My work can be seen on Bellevue Arts Museum's Facebook page.

Videography

I've been creating short films since high school, but I've been able to take my hobby to a professional level in my role at the Museum. I've created several video interviews with various artists, taken trips to artist studios to capture their work in action, created time lapse videos of artist installations, and even a stop motion animation in collaboration with New York-based fiber artist Nathan Vincent.
